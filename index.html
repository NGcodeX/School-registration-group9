Who We Are

We are a team of former college admissions officers, high school counselors, and education technology experts who have come together to create the ultimate tool for simplifying the college application process.

Frustrated by the complexity and stress that students and families face when applying to college, we set out to build a better solution. Our app combines all the essential features and guidance you need to navigate the admissions journey with confidence. From researching schools and managing deadlines, to crafting compelling essays and submitting applications, our comprehensive platform is designed to keep you organized, informed, and on track every step of the way.
